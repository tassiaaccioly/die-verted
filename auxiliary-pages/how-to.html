HOW TO PLAY

This is a narrative story with multiple endings where you are a detective trying to solve a space murder.

On each page you'll be given a context of what is happening and two or three options to chose. This will lead you to another page with the same dynamics.

Your objective is to get to the final. Any of them.

Attention!

This game doesn't save! Once you close the window your progress is gone forever.
