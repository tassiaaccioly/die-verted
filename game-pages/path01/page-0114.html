Kirlian Krizik: They were looking for someone to assemble a crew to transport some cargo from the border of the Osu quadrant to the Main Imperial Quadrant. The ad asked for a dragonfly class ship and if the crew had military background they would pay extra credits. Me being a veteran pilot from the Ak'rabahr war had several friends who were in need of come credits. I called some of them and assembled the crew.

When I contacted them to say I had the crew, the ADACorp's person said I'd have to let my ship with them for a week prior to the transport. They needed to check my ship for security breaches and malfunctions. The cargo was too important and needed to be secure.
