You exit the room and go straight to the police port where they're maintaining the ship. You ask to see the cargo, and the person says they never even removed the cargo from the ship.

You enter the ship. The air seems heavy inside. The smell of death comes right into your nose. You walk to the hangar where the cargo is supposed to be. You can see a few enforced graphene boxes poorly lit by the few lights the police put in the ship.

You come closer to one of the boxes and you can hear a low hum coming from them. You don't actually have the code for the boxes, but one of them seems to have been open.

You walk to that one. You open the lid and a strong light comes into your eyes. It blinds you for a second and right after you can hear a purr. You look inside the box and there lies what seems an animal. Very fluffy, sleeping on top of a pillow. You get the little animal, it has a very fluffy tail that wiggles softly when you take it in your arms. It also vibrates a little, letting out a purr.

You instantly feel happier. What's that? How can it make me happy?
