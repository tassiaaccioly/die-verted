You get to the archive and you don't even need to punch any numbers, the machines inside the archive seem to know exactly what to do and bring you the object.

Before it reaches you, you can feel the power emanating from it. It vibrates, and it seems to synch with your body. You feel powerful.

Once you finally touch it, it starts to melt away into pure liquid darkness. The liquid-like material spreads through the floor and enters your skin. You feel the entire universe and you remember being imprisoned for ages on end trying to call for humans to bring you your body. Humans were insignificant enough to pass unnoticed.

You are finally back. The darkness that you are spreads over land and atmo, to the universe itself. And you can see all the chaos and destruction you're causing and cannot stop yourself from smiling.

You now remember all the atrocities you committed in past lives, you remember being a god, and old god, ages and ages ago. Killing bugs was your favorite sport. You've been just waiting to awaken from the slumber.

And it feels incredible.
