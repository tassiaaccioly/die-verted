You dream. A dark dream. You feel stuck into something… something… gooey. You crawl on the ground, and you feel surrounded by nothingness, by the vastitude of the space. You remember of a time before time, when men and stars were one and you were filled with emptiness.

Something. Something crawling in the dark. Something behind you, it can almost touch you. Creepy eyes that follow you around. You feel alone and observed. You feel like you'll never be happy again. There's no escape. there's no escaping the...

CONNECTING… CONNECTING… CONNECTING

From your slumber you take ages to understand the Comm is trying to connect with you. You wake up and check the Comm. It has 21 missed connections. All from your mother. You call them:

They sound like they've been crying and seem relieved when they hear you. They want to know if you're ok. They say something about a weird event and seeing rocks and dirt. You can't quite understand them, but they say you should turn on the television. You turn it on and you can see the enormous crater that now lies where the police precinct should be. About one kilometer in diameter, everything vanished with it. You can see rocks and dirt in the crater. Something you've never seen in your life, and suddenly you realize:

There's no more precinct. No more work. No more case. No more game.
