You wake up well rested. In your fridge you find some fast regenerative rations (famously known as regenex) that taste like strawberries. You mentally thank your friend Marcus for insisting on you taking the regenexes at the end of his party and make a mental note to take some time this week to buy some rations.

The strawberry regenex isn't your favorite but it's all you have right now, so you slurp one and take another one for the road. You walk out the door and pass through the building security that ensures you're a tenant. You live in a government issued apartment, so security is always there. As you come to the elevator that will take you to the shuttle station you receive a connection. It's Rita, your partner from the police station. They say the police station just vanished along with all things in a kilometer diameter.

You turn and on the screen behind you you can see the crater in the ground where the precinct used to be. Several policepeople, firepeople and other authorities are on site. You can see rocks and dust in the hole, something you've never seen in your life.

There's no more precinct. No more work. No more case. No more game.
