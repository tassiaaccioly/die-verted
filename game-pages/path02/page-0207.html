You dream of actually going to watch "Trial by Combat Lawyer" live. It has always been a dream of you, and it's like you've always imagined. The stadium is packed with people from all places, the blinding lights pointing to the huge ring style courtroom in the middle of the stadium where the lawyers battle for his clients. One of the last earthly entertainment that survived the surge of the empire.

The crowd goes insane cheering for his favourites and you can't stop yourself from screaming alongside them. And then you see it. Your favourite lawyer, Sanction Fists, entering the courtroom. She's huge. Her interns, dressed in old timing, suits running after her, catching all the process' chips people throw her way. She's magnificent. She enters the ring like she owns the place…

CONNECTING… CONNECTING… CONNECTING

From your slumber you take ages to understand the Comm is trying to connect with you. You wake up and check the Comm. It has 21 missed connections. All from your mother. You call them:

They sound like they've been crying and seem relieved when they hear you. They want to know if you're ok. They say something about a weird event and seeing rocks and dirt. You can't quite understand them, but they say you should turn on the television. You turn it on and you can see the enormous crater that now lies where the police precinct should be. About one kilometer in diameter, everything has vanished with it. You can see rocks and dirt in the crater. Something you've never seen in your life, and suddenly you realize:

There's no more precinct. No more work. No more case. No more game.
